About Fillter

Indian stock research, made simple.

Fillter turns scattered market data into a clean, single-page dashboard — so you can understand any NSE-listed company in minutes, not hours.

What is Fillter?

Fillter is a free stock-analysis tool for India's National Stock Exchange (NSE). Search from over 2,000 listed companies and instantly get an interactive price history, year-by-year and seasonal return breakdowns, complete financial statements, and 50+ fundamental metrics — all formatted the way Indian investors expect, in ₹ Crore.

Interactive Charts

Price history from one week to all-time, with smooth zoomable trends.

Returns & Seasonality

Yearly and monthly performance, plus how a stock behaves in any given month.

Financial Statements

Income statement, balance sheet and cash flow — quarterly and annual.

Deep Fundamentals

Valuation, profitability, growth, dividends, shareholding and analyst views.

How it works

1

Search a company

Start typing a name on the home page and pick from live suggestions.

2

Fillter fetches the latest prices, financials and fundamentals from Yahoo Finance.

3

Explore the dashboard

Charts, returns, statements and metrics — all on one page, light or dark.

Data & disclaimer

Market data is provided by Yahoo Finance and may be delayed or occasionally incomplete. Fillter is for educational and informational purposes only and is not investment advice. Always do your own research before making any financial decision.
